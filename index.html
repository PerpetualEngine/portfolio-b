Why not an AI clipper

The tools got fast. They didn't get you.

AI clips in seconds. It also flattens the one thing people followed you for. The cut a beat too early, the caption on the wrong line, the voice smoothed off. Almost-right is still wrong.

The AI clipper

  • In one tested batch, roughly 40% of its clips were unusable. You sort the pile.
  • It cuts before the punchline and drops the line that made the moment land.
  • Its "virality score" routinely backs the wrong clip.
  • It has never met your brand, your niche, or the way you talk.
  • You still review, recut, recaption and reschedule. The work moved onto you.
